About DBQ Help

Experienced professionals dedicated to serving those who served

Our Mission

At DBQ Help, we believe every veteran deserves accurate, thorough, and compassionate medical evaluations. Our mission is to bridge the gap between veterans and the disability benefits they've earned through service.

We know the VA claims process can feel overwhelming. That's why we combine clinical expertise with genuine care — providing evaluations that meet VA standards while treating every veteran with the dignity and respect they deserve.

Our Team

Built by VA Professionals

Former VA Raters & Licensed Medical Examiners

DBQ Help only hires former VA Raters and other VA professionals! The M21-1 Procedure Manual for Rating is updated every Friday and our team of experts always ensures you're getting the most up to date information!

Our team is growing to serve more veterans. What stays constant: every member brings direct VA experience and a commitment to the veterans we serve.
